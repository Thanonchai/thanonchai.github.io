In the Beginning...

01 Feb, 2021

“In the beginning was the Word, and the Word was with God, and the Word was God. The same was in the beginning with God.”" — John 1: 1-14 KJV

I was inspired to have a blog for a while now. But in the process I also wanted to personalize it by not being bound by a certain internet platform. I like the idea of freedom and have been tired of certain restraints, that’s why I chose to do it this way.

I also found it a bit boring if I have to start it with a typical Hello World kind of post. I found the quote to be more powerful, while also a good sign of starting strong. You have no idea how I have been wanting to write (or type) it LOL.
